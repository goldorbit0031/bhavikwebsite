About

Building the operating layer for physical gold

I am Bhavik Korat, CEO of Gold Orbit — a company building end-to-end infrastructure for the global gold supply chain across West Africa, India, and the UAE.

Gold is a $13 trillion asset class. Yet the infrastructure connecting mines, refineries, traders, and markets remains fragmented, opaque, and largely undigitized. That gap is what Gold Orbit is built to close.

My focus is on the ground-level execution of what others only theorize: gold traceability, ASM mining digitization, equipment leasing, ERP for gold operators, and building the Africa–India–UAE gold corridor.

What I Build
01
GorbitX ERP

The first ERP system built specifically for gold mining operators — from ASM to LSM. Offline-capable. Mobile-first. Built for low-connectivity environments in West Africa.

02
Gold Traceability

AUXE system — end-to-end gold traceability from the mine to the final market. Compliance-ready. Audit-friendly. Built for the next generation of responsible gold sourcing.

03
ASM Digitization

Bringing artisanal and small-scale mining operations into the formal digital economy. Production tracking, compliance, and reporting — built for miners, not engineers.

04
Equipment Leasing

Gold mining equipment access for ASM operators who cannot afford purchase. Leasing infrastructure that unlocks productivity without capital barriers.

05
Gold Trading Support

Verified, traceable gold from compliant sources — connecting West African supply with UAE and Indian demand through a structured, transparent trading framework.

06
Investor Dashboard

Real-time visibility into Gold Orbit operations, traction, and asset performance — built for institutional investors, HNIs, and Series A stakeholders.

Gold Orbit

One connected ecosystem for physical gold — from extraction to exchange

Gold Orbit is not a trading app or a fintech layer. It is operational infrastructure — the pipes, the systems, and the compliance architecture that the global gold industry is missing.

We are building across six verticals simultaneously: ERP, traceability, equipment leasing, trading support, refining expansion, and investor dashboards. Each vertical reinforces the other. The result is a defensible, full-stack gold ecosystem.

The tagline is not marketing. It is the business model.
